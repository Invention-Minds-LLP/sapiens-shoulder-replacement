Understanding Shoulder Surgery

What Is Shoulder Surgery?

Shoulder Surgery is a specialized Orthopedic treatement for conditions affecting the shoulder joint,rotator cuff,tendons,liaments and surrounding soft tissues.

It is used to manage problems that shoulder cause pain,stiffness,weakness or restricted arm movement.

The goal of shoulder surgery is to relieve pain,restore shoulder movement and improve arm strength using safe and effective treatement tecniques. Depending on the condition, surgery may include rotator cuff repair, shoulder artroscopy, shoulder stabilization, or joint repair procedures.

Not all shoulder problems need surgery. A detailed evaluation at a specialized orthopedic clinic helps decide whether non-surgical shoulder treatement or surgery is required.

Symptoms - When to visit a Shoulder Surgeon?

You should consult a shoulder surgeon in Bangalore if you experience.

Persistent shoulder pain or discomfort

Difficulty lifting the arm or overhead movements

Shoulder stiffness or frozen shoulder symptoms

Weakness or instability of the shoulder

Pain during sleep or while lying on the affected side

Shoulder injury after a fall, accident, or sports activity

Shoulder pain affecting work or daily activities

Early consultation with Dr.Drashan Kumar A. Jain, one of the best shoulder surgeons in Banglore, helps loss of shoulder function.

Who Is Shoulder Surgery Recommed for?

Shoulder surgery may be advised for patients who:

  • Have rotator cuff tears or tendon injuries
  • Suffer from frozen shoulder not improving wit therapy
  • Experience repeated shoulder dislocation or instability
  • Have shoulder fractures or joint damaes
  • Have chronic shoulder pain not relieved by medication or physiotherapy
  • Require full shoulder function for work daily activities
